OUR VALUES

RFL was established in 2007 and the company is continuously growing since inception. Our operations are as per ISO 9001, IATF 16949 and ISO 14001. We are manufacturer of Cold Forge and Machined components for Automotive industry and are Tier-I supplier. We specialize in production of all type of high tensile fasteners, Automotive parts, Engine, Chassis, Steering, Suspension, Break Parts and Parts per drawing. Our production capacity is 18000 Tons annually and our manufacturing plant spread over a total area of 16000 Sq. Mtr. and built-up area of 12,000 Sq. Mtr. We are growing with customer support and continuous improvements.
